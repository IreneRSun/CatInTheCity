Instructions

        How To Play

        Hold the right arrow key to slowly pass a week on the clock and watch as the surroundings change to reflect the time on the clock. The clock displays the time by the month, day, and hour, from left to right respectively.

        Time Adjustment

        Time is incremented automatically as you press the right arrow key. To set the date to a desired day in the week, go to the menu for the respective options.

        Weather and Trend Information

        The webpage displays the weather information of the clock's current time automatically. For more specific weather information and information on trends, press the newspaper icon under the menu options.
